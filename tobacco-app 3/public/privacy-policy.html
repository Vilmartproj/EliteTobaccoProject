Privacy Policy

Elite Tobacco – Warehouse & Buyer Management App

Effective Date: March 31, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Elite Tobacco ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile and web application. By using this app, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. What Data We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Camera & Image Data — The app accesses your device camera to scan QR codes attached to tobacco bales and vehicle dispatches. No photos or video are captured, stored, or recorded. The camera feed is used in real time only to read QR code values.
  • Account Information — Your name, username, role (buyer, warehouse employee, or admin), and login credentials provided during registration.
  • Purchase & Transaction Data — Records of tobacco bales purchased, lot numbers, grades, weights, rates, and transaction amounts associated with your account.
  • Vehicle Dispatch Data — Dispatch IDs, QR codes scanned during loading, destination location, and dispatch status updates.
  • Device & Session Information — Basic session data (login timestamps, role-based access logs) used for security and audit purposes.

2. Why We Collect This Data

Each type of data is collected for a specific operational purpose:

  • Camera access — Solely to scan QR codes on bales and vehicle dispatch sheets. Scanning is initiated manually by the user and stops immediately after a code is detected.
  • Account information — To authenticate users, control access by role, and display personalised dashboards.
  • Transaction records — To manage tobacco purchasing, generate invoices, produce reports, and maintain an auditable history for buyers and warehouse staff.
  • Dispatch data — To track vehicle loading, confirm bale movement, and record delivery destinations.
  • Session logs — To maintain security, detect unauthorised access, and support administrative audits.

3. How We Store and Use Your Data

All data is stored on a private internal server operated by Elite Tobacco. No data is stored in any public cloud service.
  • Transaction, dispatch, and account records are stored in a secure relational database on the company server.
  • Camera feeds are processed locally on your device in real time and are never transmitted to any server.
  • Data is accessed only by authorised personnel (admins and warehouse managers) according to their assigned role.
  • The Android application enforces screenshot and screen-recording restrictions to prevent unauthorised capture of sensitive information.
  • Data is retained as long as it is needed for business records and regulatory compliance. Accounts can be deactivated by an administrator upon request.

4. Data Sharing with Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third-party companies, advertisers, or analytics services.

The app does not integrate third-party SDKs for advertising, analytics, or social media tracking. The only external libraries used are open-source tools for the user interface and QR code generation, none of which transmit user data externally.

Data may be disclosed only if required by law, a court order, or a regulatory authority, and only to the extent legally required.

5. App Permissions

  • Camera — Required for QR code scanning. Requested only when the user initiates a scan and revocable at any time in device settings.

No other sensitive device permissions (microphone, location, contacts, storage) are requested.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data, including:

  • Role-based access control — users see only the data relevant to their role.
  • Screenshot and screen-recording restrictions on the Android app.
  • Session-based authentication with server-side validation.
  • Restricted database access — only the application server can query the database.

7. Children's Privacy

This application is intended for use by authorised business personnel only. We do not knowingly collect data from individuals under the age of 18.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
